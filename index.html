Node

Node is a B2B SaaS fintech company. We provide predictive market analysis and portfolio insights, plus a 1:1 stable coin built for crypto farms and mines. We work with banks, finance and consulting teams, and quant researchers worldwide.

  • Real-time market data and predictive models
  • Stable coin, pegged 1:1
  • Withdraw or exit investments any time, no surcharges
  • We earn revenue from transactions, not from fees
